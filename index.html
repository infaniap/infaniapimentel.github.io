MY WORK RESUME CONTACT

I design wearables and embedded systems that shape real-world interaction. I build physical–digital products where UX, hardware, and system behavior need to work together seamlessly.

My work spans hardware–software integration and communication systems (BLE, NFC, WiFi, Serial), using human factors to identify interaction breakdowns and turn them into reliable, usable systems.

I'm completing an M.S. in Human Factors Engineering at Tufts University, where my thesis involves designing and validating a wearable interaction system from prototype to functional deployment.
